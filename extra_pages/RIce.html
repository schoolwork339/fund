About the Relay

On the 7th of April, St Dominic's College participated in the 2018 Relay for Life held
at the Penrith Paceway from 10am – 11pm. The students were invited to join staff for the day
as we raised funds and awareness for the Cancer Council.

The following students represented St Dominic’s they were: Brayden King, Abed Issa, Alex Robinson,
Connor Smith, Bailey Latham, Trent Hopton, Brock Stapleton, Aaron Barlow, Brody Peacock,
Riley Fivers, Matthew Handley, Hayden Walsh, Caleb Stuckey, Jonathan Mason and Nick Thornton.

The pre-relay is the opening ceremony were all the participants will get given a speech about
how the money raised will help people affected with cancer. The lap of honour is held after
the speech and is held for survivors of cancer to walk around the area with their carers.
